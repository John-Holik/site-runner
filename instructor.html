    Bank readiness

    Authoring tips

    • The correct answer is always the exact text of a choice, never a number. This tool guarantees that: you mark the answer by selecting a choice, and exports copy its text.
    • In CSV files, the choices live in one cell separated by the pipe character |, e.g. GFCI|AFCI|SPD|Fuse. Because of that, a choice cannot itself contain a | in CSV — use Export JSON for such questions.
    • Fields containing commas, quotes, or line breaks are quoted automatically on export. If you ever edit CSV by hand, wrap those fields in double quotes.
    • Keep 3–4 choices per question. 2 is allowed (but easy); more than 6 is rejected; the same choice text may not appear twice in one question.
    • why, category, and id are optional. difficulty defaults to medium. Difficulty routes questions: early checkpoints prefer easy/medium, later checkpoints prefer medium/hard.
    • Save CSV as UTF-8 (Excel: “CSV UTF-8”). Files exported here are already UTF-8.
    • The game accepts a bank only when it has at least 5 valid questions; keep it comfortably above that so questions do not repeat too quickly.
